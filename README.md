## Patient waiting times dashboard ##

#### Business Objecitves:
-Track 'current' status of patient waiting list
-Analyse historical monthly trend of waiting list in inpatient and outpatient
-Detailed specialty level and age profile analysis
####

#### Tools
-Power Bi
-Power Query
-Dax
-Excel
####

#### Key Metrics
- Average and Median waiting lists
- Current total waiting list
####

#### Insights
-The current month's waiting list is longer than the previous year's same month
- The amount of day cases and inpatients has remained relatively stable from 2018-2021, whereas the outpatients has increased steadily
- Paediatric dermatology has on average the longest wait time
